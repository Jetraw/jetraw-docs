Jetraw
======

Welcome to the Jetraw User Manual — a comprehensive guide to use the Jetraw data compression and management solutions. In this manual, you'll discover everything you need to know about both our core algorithm and the Jetraw Platform.

.. image:: _static/jetraw-main.png
   :alt: Jetraw by Dotphoton
   :width: 800px
   :align: center


Jetraw Core Algorithm:
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Programmatic Integration: Learn how to incorporate our core algorithm into your projects seamlessly.

- Compression & Decompression: Find detailed instructions on using our DLLs and SDKs designed to handle large raw image datasets efficiently.

Jetraw Platform:
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Advanced Data Management: Understand how our platform leverages Jetraw compression for faster data transfer and efficient management of large datasets.

- Unified Data Access: Explore how to send data to various storage systems while maintaining a single, accessible point for your data.

- Versatile Interfaces: Choose the tool that best fits your workflow—whether it's the desktop application, CLI, or Python SDK.

This manual is structured to provide step-by-step guidance, best practices, and troubleshooting tips, ensuring that both developers and data managers can fully harness the power of Jetraw. Dive in to explore detailed instructions, expert recommendations, and everything else you need to maximize your productivity with our state-of-the-art solutions.
